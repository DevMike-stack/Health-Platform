About Us

We are a health platform that provides a safe and secure environment for people to share their health data with their healthcare providers. We are aiming to provide a platform where people can share their health data with their healthcare providers in a safe and secure manner. Our organization seeks to offer accessible and reasonably priced healthcare services in Africa to individuals who are unwell, elderly, affected by war or conflict, or experiencing endemic outbreaks. Our team comprises medical professionals, administrative staff, and logisticians, and we operate under the guidelines set forth by the African Medicines Agency (African Union).
